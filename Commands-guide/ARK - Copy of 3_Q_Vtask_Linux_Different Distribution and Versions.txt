Q1	WHAT IS THE ROLE OF DISTRIBUTION VENDOR OF LINUX?

ANS	The role of a distribution vendor of Linux is to participate in and create community-powered upstream projects, integrate them and stabilize products platform and solutions.
Q2	What is Fedora?

ANS	Fedora is Linux Distribution, it is community driven linux distro.


Q3	Which bodies manage the Fedora projects?

ANS	Red hat is their primary sponsors, Fedora council and Fedora Engineering Steering Committee (FESCo) are other two
